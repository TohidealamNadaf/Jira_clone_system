PADDING SPECIFICATION EXPLAINED
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

padding: 1.5rem 2rem;

Breaking this down:
  1.5rem    = 24 pixels (top and bottom padding)
  2rem      = 32 pixels (left and right padding)

This creates consistent spacing:
  - 24px breathing room from navbar (professional look)
  - 32px content margins for left/right (aligns with design system)
