ISSUE
─────
Error when saving budget: "Unknown validation rule: size"

ROOT CAUSE & FIX
────────────────
File: src/Controllers/TimeTrackingController.php (Line 367)
Problem: Used unsupported validation rule 'size:3'
Solution: Changed to 'min:3|max:3' (both are valid rules)

BEFORE:
  'currency' => 'required|size:3'

AFTER:
  'currency' => 'required|min:3|max:3'

WHY CHANGED
───────────
The Validator class doesn't have a 'size' rule. But 'min' and 'max' work
perfectly for validating exactly 3-character currency codes:
  - min:3 ensures at least 3 characters
  - max:3 ensures at most 3 characters
  - Together: exactly 3 characters ✓
