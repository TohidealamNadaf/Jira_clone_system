───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  STEP 1: CLOSE ALL BROWSER WINDOWS (Critical!)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. Close ALL tabs with the app
2. Close the ENTIRE browser completely
3. Wait 10 seconds
4. Open browser again (fresh)

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  STEP 2: CLEAR CACHE (Critical!)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Windows (Chrome, Firefox, Edge):
  1. Press: CTRL + SHIFT + DEL (all 3 together)
  2. Time range: Change to "All time" (dropdown)
  3. Check BOTH boxes:
     ☑ Cookies and other site data
     ☑ Cached images and files
  4. Click: "Clear data" or "Delete"
  5. Wait for success message
  6. Close the clear dialog

Mac (Safari):
  1. Click menu: Safari → Preferences
  2. Click tab: "Privacy"
  3. Click button: "Manage Website Data..."
  4. Find: "localhost"
  5. Click: "Remove"
  6. Click: "Done"

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  STEP 3: HARD REFRESH (Critical!)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Windows:
  Press: CTRL + F5 (hold CTRL, press F5)
  OR: CTRL + SHIFT + R

Mac:
  Press: CMD + SHIFT + R
  OR: CMD + Option + R

Wait: Full page load (status bar 100%)
